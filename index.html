About

I make things work. And feel right.

I'm a game developer with experience across solo projects, team productions, and professional studio work. My focus is always on the mechanics layer: how things move, respond, and feel in the player's hands.

Building DishDash as a solo developer meant owning every decision from the first prototype to the Steam release. That process pushed me to think clearly about scope, prioritise ruthlessly, and ship something real.

I've worked across PC, VR, and mobile, and I'm always looking for the next problem worth solving.
