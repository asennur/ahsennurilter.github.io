About the Game

DishDash is a fast-paced time management simulation game where players step into the shoes of a restaurant worker juggling orders, customers, and kitchen chaos. With each level the pace intensifies — demanding quick thinking and precise execution.

The game was entirely solo-developed: every mechanic, customer interaction system, UI/UX element, piece of art, and audio cue was designed and implemented by a single developer. AI tools were used to accelerate production, allowing a one-person team to reach a professional-quality release.

DishDash launched on Steam with over 600 wishlists prior to full release, demonstrating strong organic interest from the time management and simulation gaming community.
