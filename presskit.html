About the Game

DishDash is a fast-paced time management simulation where players step into a busy restaurant kitchen — prepping dishes, serving customers, and managing resources before the clock runs out. Every level turns up the heat, demanding quick thinking and precise execution.

The game was built entirely solo: every mechanic, customer interaction system, UI element, piece of art, and audio cue was designed and coded by a single developer. AI tools were leveraged to speed up production, letting a one-person team hit a polished release standard.

The result: 600+ Steam wishlists before the full launch — built entirely on organic discovery.
